How you get paid

  • Dispatch fee — 5% of the line haul. That's it. Charged only on the line haul.
  • Accessorials go 100% to your truck — nothing skimmed:
  • Fuel surcharge — currently $0.71 / mile
  • Hazmat — $100
  • Stop charges — $50 per extra stop
  • You share your truck & trailer number so Landstar can track the load and your location.
  • You control your status — go available or offline anytime.

Example: on a $1,000 load with ~$200 in accessorials, the line haul is $800 — your dispatch fee is $40, and you keep the rest plus every accessorial dollar.
